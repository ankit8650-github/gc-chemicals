T-1 GREY ECOFIX TILES ADHESIVE ( ETA )

GC ECOFIX ( T1 GREY ) ADHESIVE IS A POLYMER BASED ADHESIVE FOR FIXING POROUS CLAY & CERAMIC TILES, HIGH POROSITY TILES AND QUARRY TILES OF REGULAR SIZE. IT IS SUITABLE FOR BOTH HORIZONTAL AND VERTICAL SURFACE. IT IS USE ON DRY AREA INTERIOR FLOOR & INTERIOR WALL UP TO A HEIGHT OF 3 MTRS, OVER THE CEMENTATIONS SUBSTRATE FOR INDOOR APPLICATION, IN DRY AS WELL AS WET AREAS FLOOR. COVERAGE: APPROX - 5 TO 5.5 Square meter / 20 KG BAG AT 3 MM BED THICKNESS

Application & Usage Guidelines

1

Area of Applications

Suitable for flooring and walls in various internal and external applications.

  • Apply on flooring and walls for internal applications.
  • Can be used in external, dry & wet areas, domestic and commercial applications.
  • Joint width as per architect/engineer recommendation.
  • Always use grout (Normal & Epoxy Grout) between tiles/stones.
  • Remove spacers once adhesive is set firm.
2

Precautions

Important considerations to ensure safety and durability of the installation.

  • Use only on concrete surfaces, cement plaster, or brick surfaces.
  • Do not apply directly on gypsum plaster, boards, plywood, laminates, metals, or plastics.
  • Do not use for artificial/nano/engineered stone, composites, or metal tiles.
  • Do not add excess water, sand, or cement at site.
  • Protect from direct rainfall and foot traffic for at least 24 hours.
3

Application Instructions

Ensure the surface and adhesive application is done correctly for best results.

  • Surface must be sufficiently flat to achieve specified tiling flatness.
  • Suitable for expected service conditions.
  • Surfaces must be dry, clean, free from loose material and contamination.
  • Surface should be cleaned and roughened before tile/stone installation.
4

How to Mixing

Proper mixing ensures workability and strength of the adhesive.

  • Use approx. 5 – 5.5 L of water for 20 kg of powder.
  • Place clean potable water into a clean mixing bowl before adding powder.
  • Adjust water for desired consistency but do not exceed recommended limit.
  • Leave mixture for 5 minutes before use, then remix for 30 seconds.
  • Allow adhesive to polymerize for 2–5 minutes before application.

Note: Coverage will vary depending on trowel notch size, tile type/size, and substrate smoothness. Information is given in good faith based on R&D. Company reserves the right to update instructions without notice.
