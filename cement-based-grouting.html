Application & Usage Guidelines

1

Area of Applications

  • Floor and walls
  • Internal & external area
  • Domestic applications
  • Commercial applications
  • Heavily trafficked floors
  • Movement / vibration areas
  • Swimming pools
2

Precautions

  • Always apply on completely dry tile joints.
  • Do not apply on wet or damp tile joints.
  • Do not add too much water to the grout.
  • Always mix grout in a clean pot.
  • Do not add a different colour to the grout.
  • While cleaning, frequently change the water.
  • Change cleaning cloth/sponge when they are impregnated with grout.
  • Do not allow water to flow or run into un-grouted joints during application.
  • Allow foot traffic on applied area only after 24 hours.
  • Do not use acid to clean joints. Use GC Tile Cleaner for cleaning purpose.
3

Application Instructions

  • Always fill grout after 24 hours of tile installation.
  • Ensure there is no dust or debris between the tile joints while grouting.
  • Fill the grout at least 3 mm deep.
  • Before filling the grout, make sure there is no moisture between the tiles.
  • Use masking tape if the tiles or stones are very rough.
4

Grout Cleaning

  • Within 35–45 minutes of grout filling, clean the tile joints with a clean cloth or sponge.
  • Wet the cloth or sponge lightly in water before cleaning.
  • Do not press too hard on the grouted area while cleaning.
  • Do not use any type of acid when cleaning the grout.
  • The hardened adhesive can be removed from tools mechanically.
  • Note: Use caution when polishing soft glazed tiles or polished stone.
5

How to Mixing

  • Take pre-measured water in a clean mixing vessel.
  • Add CEMENT POLYMER GROUT to the water and mix until a lump-free, smooth paste of uniform colour is obtained.
  • Allow the mix to slake for 2–5 minutes, then remix.
  • Do not reconstitute the mixed grout by adding more water, as this will reduce the final strength.
  • Do not add excess water.
  • Use the paste within 40–50 minutes from mixing time.
