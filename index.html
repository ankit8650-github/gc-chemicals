Our Expertise

Premium tile and stone fixing solutions backed by technical excellence and proven performance

01

Comprehensive Tile Adhesive Solutions

Full range of polymer-modified, cement-based tile adhesives (T-1 to T-5) for every application—from basic interior ceramic tiles to high-performance exterior and vitrified tiles.

02

Advanced Grouting Systems

Complete grouting solutions including cement-based joint fillers, high-performance epoxy grout, and polymer additives for enhanced flexibility and durability.

03

Waterproofing & Protection

Advanced waterproofing solutions and protective coatings that safeguard structures from water damage, leakage, and environmental wear.

04

Specialty Stone Installation

Expert solutions for natural stones, marble, glass mosaics, and designer cladding with specialized white adhesives for light-colored materials.

05

Surface Care & Maintenance

Professional tile cleaners and maintenance projects that effectively remove stains, dirt, and grime while preserving surface integrity.

06

Bonding Enhancement

Cement bonder additives that significantly strengthen traditional cement-sand mortar for superior tile fixing and plastering applications.
